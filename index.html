Science Summary Landing Page

Welcome to the Science Summary Library! Here we have collected the research of many research groups and made their research accessible to everyone.

Explore easy-to-read summaries on many different topics. Use the quick links to access specific fields directly or search by keyword to find summaries of interest.

How to search?

This page offers several ways to find out about new and interesting topics.

1. Using quick links

Using the quick links below you can directly access a wide range of research projects related to the topic of interest. Simply click on the link and you will be forwarded to the collection that interests you most

2. Keyword search

Our summary collection can be filtered using keywords entered by the authors themselves. For this you can go to our Summary Collection and select the keywords that interest you most.
